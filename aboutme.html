About Me

Hi there! Thanks for stopping by.

My day usually begins with a loose plan that quickly takes shape: a Pomodoro timer on my browser, sticky notes with time estimates in curly brackets, and big tasks broken into smaller steps so I can see what might get in the way before it does. It’s a rhythm that lets me work steadily and adjust without losing sight of quality.

I often find myself reading what people share on platforms like X (Twitter), Reddit, and even YouTube comment sections. It’s like a hidden treasure, seeing how so many different backgrounds and life experiences shape their views on the same topic. Sometimes it’s amusing, sometimes surprising, but it always reminds me how quickly we jump to judge things and how those judgments rarely capture the full picture. What really matters are the perspectives we exchange as people trying to understand each other.

You’ll also find me exploring trending articles and courses to keep my skills sharp and my workflow efficient.

Movement is my go-to power-up: exercise reinvigorates my body, clears the fog in my mind, and fuels me with lasting energy. Pair that with daily desk-hygyne routine, quiet moments of prayer, mindful breathwork, and the occasional indulgent bite of Tres Leches cake, that’s my recipe for recharging and showing up fully.

Beyond work, my days are balanced with hydration, wholesome home-cooked meals, fruits and nuts, good sleep, 4-5 pages of reading, aimeless random conversations, table tennis or hard court tennis, laughter, and time with family.
