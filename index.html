Welcome to my space.

I’m passionate about exploring the spaces where UX processes and developer constraints intersect, especially where one influences or limits the other. I’m curious about the real-world dynamics between PMs, UX professionals, and developers, and I aim to bring my skill sets, communication, and system design thinking capabilities to help better align and create stronger outcomes.

I’m equally driven to deeply understand a customer base, combining behavioral insights with qualitative and quantitative data to reveal their real product experiences. My goal is to identify both obvious and subtle experience gaps, using evidence to inform solutions rather than assumptions.

I am excitited to work alongside AI using my abilities in the most effective way and rethink the nature of gaps and make world as inclusive and accessible as possible not just through end-products but by imbibing processes through inclusive thinking.
